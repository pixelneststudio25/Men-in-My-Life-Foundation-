About Our Foundation

Men In My Life Foundation is a registered ministry dedicated to strengthening men spiritually, emotionally, and relationally.

A Vision Birthed in 1999, Active Since 2015

Founded on divine inspiration, our ministry began as an online outreach in 2015 and was officially registered in 2017. We focus on equipping men to fulfill their God-given roles in family, church, and society.

Through prayer networks, mentorship programs, and community outreach, we've impacted thousands of lives across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Vision: 4 Generations of Righteousness

Building a legacy of faith that transcends generations, transforming families and communities.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Men In My Life Foundation

QUEEN ESTHER NAILAH Yakubu

Visionary & Spiritual Leader

With a divine calling received in 1999, our founder has been instrumental in bringing the vision of Men In My Life Foundation to reality. Her passion for building righteous men and strong families stems from a deep conviction that transformed lives lead to transformed communities.

Through years of prayer, dedication, and unwavering faith, she established this ministry as an online outreach in 2015 and oversaw its official registration in 2017. Her leadership continues to inspire thousands of men and women across Nigeria and beyond.

Her commitment to the "4 Generations of Righteousness" vision is reflected in every aspect of the ministry's work, from prayer networks to mentorship programs and community outreach initiatives.

"When God gives you a vision for the men in your life, it's not just for today. It's a legacy that will impact generations to come."

— Founder, Men In My Life Foundation
